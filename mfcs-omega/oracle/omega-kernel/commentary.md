# OMEGA Commentary

OMEGA is the decision kernel that stands on MFCS: it never outruns the
envelope laws and never escapes the invariants. Every move is traceable,
bounded, and reconstructible from logs and surfaces.

# OMEGA Canon

1. **No move without a trace.**
2. **No trace without an envelope.**
3. **No envelope without an invariant.**
4. **No invariant without a witness.**

# OMEGA Litany

- Sense, but do not drift.
- Frame, but do not distort.
- Evaluate, but do not excuse.
- Select, but do not conceal.
- Act, but do not forget.

# OMEGA Benediction

May every decision surface be:
- bounded by law,
- illuminated by trace,
- and legible to the next operator.
